sun

Sun represents outside, extroverted aspect of self. Sun represents the more 'masculine' aspects of the self such as your ego and outer consciousness. It represents your willpower, strength, vitality, life force, how you deal with situations in life. Your Sun sign also talks about your interests and active awareness.

Sun in Aries

  • Aries is the first sign of the zodiac, which means that it carries quite a few child-like traits such as self-centeredness and selfishness.
  • Very direct and forward when it comes to pursuing their love interest.
  • People who very easily "fall in love at first sight" but then move on very quickly.
  • Get bored very easily.
  • Tend to make fun of people they fancy.
  • Impulsive and impatient
  • They get obsessed with the idea of someone very quickly and then forget about it when the passion dies out for them

  • They like people who are very direct to them and don't play games as they need to know they have your full attention, because if they don't they'll go get it from somewhere else
  • They can pick fights very quickly but also drop them very quickly and just let it go
  • Huge egos: if you break up with them, they might be hurt just because they got broken up with not because they really liked them
  • They need exciting relationships
  • If they get bored and passion dies for them, they might cheat
  • Upbeat and optimistic
